LabPass - Scan to login. Tap to logout.

LabPass replaces manual lab computer sign-ins with a secure handoff between the Chrome extension on the lab PC and your phone PWA.

How it works Open PWA

For lab desktops and PCs

Install the LabPass Chrome extension, open the extension popup, and let it show the QR for students to scan from the PWA.

Install Chrome extension

How it works

01

Install extension on Chrome

The lab computer must have the LabPass Chrome extension installed and pinned.

02

Approve in the PWA

Students open the PWA on phone, scan the extension QR, and approve login.

03

Logout when done

Tap logout from the PWA. Extension idle fallback clears cookies if needed.
