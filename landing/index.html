LabPass replaces manual lab computer sign-ins with a secure handoff between the Chrome extension on the shared PC and your phone.

Open Mobile App Download Chrome Extension

(Downloads a ZIP. Extract it and load the /extension folder in Chrome's Developer Mode)

1

Install Extension

The lab computer must have the LabPass Chrome extension installed and pinned.

2

Approve in App

Students open the PWA on their phone, scan the extension QR, and approve the login.

3

Logout Seamlessly

Tap logout from the PWA. Extension idle fallback clears all cookies if you forget.
