HOW TO USE

01

Setup & Prep

Install the Chrome extension on the shared lab PC. Launch the PWA on your phone and sign in with Google to establish your secure identity.

02

Scan to Connect

Click the extension icon on the PC to reveal the secure QR code. Scan the code using the scanner inside the mobile PWA to initiate the handoff.

03

Approve & Logout

Confirm the connection request on your phone to log in instantly. When finished, simply tap logout from the mobile PWA to clear all sessions.

Chrome Extension Installation Guide

  1. Download & Extract: Click the button above to download the ZIP file, then extract it on your computer.
  2. Open Extensions: In Chrome, open a new tab and navigate to chrome://extensions.
  3. Developer Mode: Toggle the Developer mode switch in the top-right corner to active.
  4. Load Unpacked: Click the Load unpacked button in the top-left header.
  5. Select Folder: Choose the extracted extension folder inside the ZIP files, nothing else.
